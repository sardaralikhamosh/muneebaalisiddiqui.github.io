About Me

Pakistani Cricketer - History Maker

Hello! I'm Muneeba Ali, a professional cricketer from Pakistan. I'm passionate about cricket and dedicated to representing my country with pride and excellence. My journey in cricket has been filled with challenges and triumphs, and I continue to strive for improvement in every match.

I made history in February 2023 by becoming the first Pakistani woman to score a T20 International century, achieving this milestone during the ICC Women's T20 World Cup. I scored 102 runs off 68 balls against Ireland, creating not only the first T20I century for Pakistan but also the first for any Pakistani woman in a T20 World Cup.

As an athlete, I believe in discipline, hard work, and sportsmanship. I'm committed to inspiring the next generation of cricketers, especially young girls who dream of pursuing sports professionally.
